! How to Install the App Easy steps for your platform

Android (Chrome)

Tap the menu
and select "Add to Home screen". You’ll have offline access and app-like experience.

iOS (Safari)

Tap the button in Safari’s toolbar
then tap "Add to Home Screen".
Your app will launch standalone.

iOS (Non-Safari browsers)

To install the app, please open this site in Safari first,
then follow the steps above.

Desktop Browsers

Look for the icon near the address bar, or click here: Install App
